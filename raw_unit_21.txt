21 Music
Downloading and managing your musicA
Downloading music as MP31 files is easy.
• You can download a whole album or individual tracks2 onto your phone or computer.
• You can save your favourite albums and tracks in playlists so you can find them easily.
• You can sync3 (or, more formal, synchronise) the music files on your computer with your phone.
• You can also stream4 music from the Internet or from your computer to other rooms in your
home if you have the right equipment.

Common mistakes
We say: classical music. (NOT classic) She loves classical music. (NOT classic music) Music is
uncountable. They played fantastic music for an hour. (NOT fantastic musics)
