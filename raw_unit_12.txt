Everyday minor problems

Things that go wrong in houses and flats
This pipe's leaking.
He kicked the football too hard and it smashed a window.
This pot is cracked.
The batteries in this torch have run out / are dead.
I'm sorry, your cup's chipped.
The lights aren't working - there's been a power cut.
The door handle's come off / fallen off.
The washing machine broke down the other day.
The biscuits got crushed in my bag.
My computer keeps crashing today.

Everyday minor injuries
She twisted her ankle coming down the stairs.
I bumped / banged my head against the cupboard door and got a bruise.
Kelly tripped and fell down and grazed her knee this morning.

Other everyday minor problems
I've mislaid my memory stick.
She spilt some coffee on the carpet. I hope it doesn't leave a stain.
The sink is blocked.
I'm afraid I've dented your car.
My mouse has stopped working; it could be a software problem.
I've locked myself out.
The car won't start. Perhaps the battery's flat.
The kitchen clock's slow / fast / stopped.

Language help
These words can be used as verbs or nouns:
flood, crack, chip, bump, bang, bruise, graze, dent, stain, lock.
